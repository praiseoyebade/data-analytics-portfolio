# Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard
## Project Overview

This project involved the development of an interactive Financial Performance Analysis Dashboard in Power BI to provide a comprehensive view of the company's financial health and business performance.

The dashboard was designed to help decision-makers monitor key financial metrics, including revenue, profitability, assets, customer segments, regional performance, and operational trends.

By transforming raw financial data into meaningful visualizations and KPIs, the report enables stakeholders to identify growth opportunities, evaluate business performance, and make data-driven decisions.

## Project Objectives

To create a centralized financial reporting dashboard that enables business leaders to:

Monitor overall financial performance.
Track profitability and key financial KPIs.
Analyze regional and customer segment performance.
Identify high-performing product categories.
Evaluate financial health and operational efficiency.
Support strategic and data-driven decision-making.

## Business Problem

The company needed a centralized reporting solution to monitor financial performance across revenue, profitability, assets, customer segments, and regional operations.

Decision-makers lacked a single view of key financial metrics and required insights to identify profitable business areas, monitor growth trends, and support strategic planning.

## Process

### Data Preparation
Imported and reviewed financial datasets.
Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query.
Standardized fields and prepared data for analysis.
### Data Modeling

Created a structured data model for reporting and analysis.
### KPI Development
Developed measures for:
Total Revenue
Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
Gross Profit
Net Profit
Gross Profit Margin
Net Profit Margin
Debt-to-Assets Ratio
Return on Assets (ROA)
### Dashboard Development
Designed an Executive Summary page for leadership reporting.
Built Financial Performance, Balance Sheet, and Transaction Detail pages.
Added interactive filters for Year, Quarter, Segment, and Region.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Performance
The business generated $69M in revenue and $14M in gross profit.
### Profitability
Net profit was $4M, resulting in a 5.1% net profit margin.
Profitability remains positive, although operating costs reduce overall margins.
### Customer Segments
Corporate customers contributed approximately 50% of total sales, making them the primary revenue driver.
### Regional Performance
The South and West regions held the highest concentration of company assets and represented key operational areas.
### Product Category Analysis
Technology generated approximately $10.4M in profit, significantly outperforming all other categories.
### Financial Health
Debt-to-Assets Ratio of 33.7% indicates moderate financial leverage.
Return on Assets of 7.4% demonstrates effective utilization of company assets.

## Recommendations

1. Improve Cost Efficiency

Review operational expenses and procurement costs to increase net profit margins.

2. Invest in High-Performing Categories

Expand investment in Technology products due to their strong contribution to profitability.

3. Strengthen Corporate Customer Relationships

Prioritize retention and growth strategies for Corporate clients, who generate the largest share of revenue.

4. Leverage High-Performing Regions

Continue strategic investment in the South and West regions while assessing growth opportunities in lower-performing regions.
